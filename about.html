Survive the Storm

About the Game

Survive the Storm is a survival game made by Christoph Marko with pixi.js.

The Goal of the game is to stay alife as long as possible and avoid falling into the lava. Therefore, you have to dodge the obstacles flying in your direction. Through not pushing any button you can rest. With this you can fill up your power, with which you can stop the time of the game.
